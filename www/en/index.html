DELECTABLY Indian

The letters "N" and "I" in the word "Nila" represents the fusion of
Norwegian and Indian... a rendezvous between ancient Indian tastes
and traditions in symbiosis with Norwegian ingredients. Our guests will
dine in an elegant blue/gold inspired ambience. The word Nila means
also exactly that, "blue", the colour which is often found in nature and
around us, representing a new beginning and holding a particular
significance in Indian culture and heritage.
